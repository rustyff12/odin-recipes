Description

Verily, this recipe for roasted chicken doth bring to mind the method my grandmother employed. The meat doth turn out most juicy, and 'tis a pleasure to partake of the celery once cooked.

Indeed, roasted chicken is a dish that doth always impress. Yet, its preparation is simple, fitting for both novice and skilled cooks alike. This recipe for juicy roasted chicken is a true delight for all.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Butter
  • Celery

Steps

  1. Season the chicken inside and out.
  2. Add the butter to the chicken and the pan.
  3. Stuff the cavity with celery.
  4. Bake until the chicken is fully roasted.
